About Me

Living an active sport-filled life is important to me. I've always thrived in a team environment playing basketball for Connacht and Galway in secondary school. Today my hobbies are running, tag rugby, and hockey. So much of who I am as a professional comes from what I learnt through sports and being part of a team. I see this most when motivating a team during a particularly stressful project as the deadline approaches, highlighting what is going well but more importantly where we as a team can improve to achieve our collective goal.

How I Work

I work best in a team environment but only when the work that I am doing directly impacts the team in a positive way. The work that I do has to matter to the team, administrative tasks do not excite me. I like to lead the team, lead the conversation, I have many ideas on how to improve a process and like to brainstorm with others on how to make sure the process works for everyone. I don't like it when my ideas are not considered or fall on deaf ears.

I am goal driven, competitive, and want to tackle issues as they arise head on. I like bullet points on the issues and don't like when information is dragged out. I constantly strive to improve myself - I realize my areas of weakness and try to improve upon them. This is most visible through all the additional learning courses that I have completed.
